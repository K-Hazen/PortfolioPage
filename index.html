My Story


Hello! Thanks for stopping by my portfolio page. I’m Kyle, in case you were so transfixed by the waterfall above that you missed my name. I am currently a student in the Eleven Fifty Academy software development program in Fishers, Indiana. After ten years in youth ministry, I decided it was time for a transition.

I have always enjoyed finding creative ways to utilize the tools that I had at my disposal to solve problems. In youth ministry, that skillset surfaced in a large variety of ways. From trouble shooting audio/visual technology to finding ways to creatively train and equip students for leadership. I got very good at using what I had to find a solution to the problem at hand. It was in those moments where I felt energized!

In college I took a basic html and database class, in which my professor said, “Hey, you’re pretty good at this!” Never in all of my academic career have I had any other teacher say that to me! It always stuck with me as a “what if” I would have tried that route.

As a result, when I began this transition developer was one of the options at the top of the list. Upon investigating what this world was all about things like creative problem solving, the detail-oriented nature of the job, and the challenge of figuring out ways to link the flow of data jumped out at me. I knew I had to give a chance, so I dove in! I am very excited to see where this career path leads.

I can’t wait to talk to you!

Some random fun stuff about me. I am an accomplished saxophonist who loves concerts, sports, and the outdoors. I am always up for a competitive table tennis match and am an avid backpacker. All the pictures you see on this page are from several trekking adventures I took in New Zealand in March 2019.
